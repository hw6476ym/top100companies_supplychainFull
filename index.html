About this map. Every node is a real company, factory, refinery, mine, well, or farm. Capacities and ownership reflect publicly disclosed figures (USGS, IEA, BloombergNEF, S&P Commodity Insights, company filings, trade press through 2026). Supplier networks shift; treat as a teaching map. ⚠ marks single-source / chokepoint dependencies.
How to read this map
Flow runs left → right: company → product → assembly → component → factory → material → refinery → mine. Companies are colored by sector; everything else by tier. Click any node to follow the chain.
